PITCH BLACK

THE AMAZONS

The Amazon's are an alternative rock band out of Reading, United Kingdom. Their music. They were formed in 2014 and have released 4 albums so far. The song "Pitch Black" is from their album "21st Century Fiction." The song was written as a cognisant realization that time is moving fast and their 20's are ending soon.
